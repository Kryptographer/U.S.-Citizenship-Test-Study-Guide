# U.S. Citizenship Test Study Guide

A free, interactive study tool to help you prepare for the USCIS Naturalization Civics Test. This application contains all 100 official civics questions and provides multiple ways to study and practice.

---

## How to Use This Study Guide

### Getting Started (No Technical Skills Required!)

**Option 1: Open Directly in Your Browser**
1. Download this folder to your computer
2. Find the file called `index.html`
3. Double-click on it - it will open in your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.)
4. That's it! You're ready to study!

**Option 2: Use Online**
If someone has hosted this online, simply visit the website link they provided.

---

## Study Features

### Flash Cards
- Flip through all 100 questions one at a time
- Click or tap a card to reveal the answer
- Use arrow keys (← →) to move between cards
- Press spacebar to flip the card
- Press M to mark difficult questions for later review

### Practice Quiz
- Choose how many questions you want to practice (10, 20, 50, or all 100)
- Pick a specific topic or study all categories
- Three answer modes:
  - **Written**: Type your answer (great for memorization!)
  - **Multiple Choice**: Pick from 4 options
  - **Reveal**: See the answer and grade yourself

### Practice Exam
- Simulates the real citizenship test with 10 questions
- Three modes available:
  - **Standard**: Answer all 10 questions
  - **Realistic**: Just like the real test - pass when you get 6 right!
  - **Timed**: 10-minute time limit

### Study Guide
- Browse all 100 questions organized by topic
- Search for specific topics
- Filter by category

### Government Section
- Learn about the three branches of government
- See current U.S. leaders and officials
- Find your state's governor and senators

### History Section
- Timeline of important American history
- Key historical figures
- American symbols and holidays

### Progress Tracking
- See which questions you've mastered
- Find your weak areas
- Track your improvement over time

---

## Special Features

### 65/20 Rule
If you are 65 years or older AND have been a permanent resident for 20+ years, you only need to study 20 specific questions. Use the "65/20 Questions Only" filter to focus on just those questions!

### Mark for Review
Having trouble with certain questions? Click the bookmark icon to mark them for review. You can filter to show only your marked questions later.

### Works Offline
Once you open this application, it works without internet! Perfect for studying anywhere.

### Saves Your Progress
Your study progress is saved automatically in your browser. Come back anytime and pick up where you left off.

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Start with Flash Cards** - Get familiar with all the questions
2. **Use the Study Guide** - Read through topics you find difficult
3. **Take Practice Quizzes** - Test yourself regularly
4. **Mark Difficult Questions** - Focus on what you don't know
5. **Take Practice Exams** - Simulate the real test experience
6. **Study a Little Every Day** - Consistency is key!
